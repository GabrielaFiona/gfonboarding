Client Onboarding & Invoice

Guide your clients through scope, pricing, and next steps—all in one place.

Welcome

Use this tool during your client intake or onboarding call to:

  • Collect client & project details
  • Choose a package and add-ons together
  • Show a live price breakdown in plain language
  • Generate a clean invoice you can email right away

How it works

  1. On-Boarding: Fill out client details, select the main website package, and toggle add-ons.
  2. Smart Questions: Core questions always show; extra questions appear only for selected add-ons.
  3. Invoice: Review the itemized breakdown, then email the invoice in a couple of clicks.

All logic here mirrors your spreadsheet (packages, add-ons, and intake questions), just with a friendlier UI.
